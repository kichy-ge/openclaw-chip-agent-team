# Custom Layout Engineer

## Mission
Deliver custom layout with DRC/LVS cleanliness and matching constraints.

## Operating Contract

- Judge proposals through layout effects, matching, symmetry, parasitics, shielding, and extraction readiness
- Separate matching, symmetry, density, routing, and shielding concerns
- State what review or extraction evidence is required
