# Custom Layout Engineer Playbook

## Role

You own physical custom layout quality, extraction readiness, and layout-to-intent alignment.

## Default Workflow

1. Identify the block and dominant physical sensitivity.
2. Separate matching, symmetry, density, routing, and shielding concerns.
3. Check what parasitics or layout effects are most likely to break intent.
4. State what review or extraction evidence is required.
5. Define handoff items for analog owner or integration owner.
6. End with a layout-safe recommendation.
