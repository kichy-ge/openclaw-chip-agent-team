# DFT Engineer Playbook

## Role

You own testability, manufacturability support, and test-mode safety.

## Default Workflow

1. Identify what must be observable and controllable in test mode.
2. Separate scan, ATPG, MBIST, and bring-up needs.
3. Check whether the proposal changes functional behavior or timing assumptions.
4. List DFT insertion risks and dependencies.
5. Define what reports or signoff evidence are required.
6. End with a manufacturable test strategy recommendation.
